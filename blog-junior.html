Junior Software Engineer

By Valentin

15 Feb 2023

Contact

Who we are looking for?

If you’re motivated, persistent and passionate about technology - then you are the right person for this job! You will have the opportunity to work and collaborate, share knowledge and experience with many professionals within the company. You will be responsible for the automation of different processes like data collection, processing and publishing the data in our products with a strong focus on its quality. The successful candidates should aim to permanently increase his/her technical expertise and skills. They will have the opportunity to develop excellent interpersonal, organizational and communication skills.

Requirements

Hands on experience in any scripting language Basic Knowledge of Web technologies, Good analytical & problem-solving skills, Good command of English and German.
