Senior Software Engineer

By Valentin

12 Jan 2023

Contact

We are looking for software engineers who are willing to shape the future with us. Constantly experimenting with different technologies, we keep ourselves up to date all the time. Right now, these are some of our favorites.

Big Data ( Spark, Kafka, Cassandra, Flink,..)

Cloud Computing ( AWS, GCP, MS Azure, On-Premises: Kubernetes, Mesos, Docker, ...)

Machine Learning (TensorFlow, Keras, Pytorch, Sci-Kit, ...)

Distributed Systems mostly implemented (but not limited to) in Java, Scala, GO, Python or R.

Who we are looking for?

University degree in Computer Science or a related field. You are a software developer at heart, proficient in programming languages like Java, JVM based languages, Python or C++. Open to dive into new languages if useful. You have a strong desire to continuously improve processes and drive the product quality. You love working in a Linux environment and are comfortable with Linux shell scripting. Experience with DevOps methods or tools, especially for Continuous Integration/Delivery or for automation and orchestration. Experiences with Cloud (e.g. AWS, Azure, Google Cloud Platform, Kubernetes, Mesos) and Linux container technologies (Docker and related orchestration frameworks), or keen to learn. Familiar with software testing, and Agile/Scrum development methodology. Learn, evaluate and incorporate new technology and methodologies on own initiative.

Requirements

Hands on experience in any scripting language Basic Knowledge of Web technologies, Good analytical & problem-solving skills, Good command of English and German.

What do we offer?

Unique career opportunity, possibility to learn, while earning. Chance to enhance technical expertise and gain new skills. Opportunity to work with an international team of highly motivated, friendly and professional people. Home office. Flexible time and a lot more!
